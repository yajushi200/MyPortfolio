About Me

Honours graduate from Centennial College with an Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering Technology specialising in Artificial Intelligence, graduating with a GPA of 4.299 out of 4.5. I am a results-driven AI Engineer & Data Analyst passionate about turning complex data into actionable intelligence.

As a Data Analyst Intern at Scriptics Technologies, I designed and deployed end-to-end ETL pipelines, Power BI dashboards, and AWS cloud workflows using Glue, S3, and Redshift. I also contributed as a Software Lab Assistant at Centennial College and previously gained industry exposure as an IT Technologist Intern handling system support and infrastructure.

My portfolio spans AI, ML, and cloud engineering — including Luminate AI, a multi-agent tutoring system built with LangGraph, LangChain, ChromaDB, and Google Gemini; a Marine Species Classifier achieving 91.82% accuracy using ResNet50 transfer learning; and an Amazon Reviews Sentiment Analysis pipeline powered by NLP and AWS cloud services.
